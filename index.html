Grumpy Cats

About Grumpy Cat

Grumpy Cat was a cute little girl cat that always looked pissed off and fed up. Her facial features were the result of a condition called dwarfism. Her actual name was Tardar Sauce.

Look at that pretty furry kitty face

Why I love grumpy-looking cats

  1. they seem to hate everybody, which is fun
  2. they are so small yet they seem so superior (which they are, of course)
  3. their disapproving little faces form a delightful contrast with their cute furry bodies
  4. have you seen the manul cat (or Pallas's cat)?

Other grumpy-looking cats

There are many more cats with angry facial features. Ever since Grumpy Cat died the world has been looking for a new icon, but so far none of the contestants have proven to be worthy successors.
